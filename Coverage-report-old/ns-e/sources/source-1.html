3 /**
4  * Besides the Generics, the loss of property of size is another difference between ArrayQueue and
5  * IntQueue. The size of ArrayQueue is calculated by the formula, as are empty status and full
6  * status.
7  *
8  * <p>ArrayQueue maximum size is data.length - 1. The place of the variable rear is always in front
9  * of the variable front logistically if regard the data array as circular. so the number of states
10  * of the combination of rear and front is the length of the data array. And one of the total states
11  * is used to be the judge if the queue is empty or full.
